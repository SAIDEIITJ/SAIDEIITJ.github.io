School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDE School)

In the context of emergence of AI and Data Science as a multi-faceted application oriented integrative technology discipline, Indian Institute of Jodhpur (IIT Jodhpur) has set up a School of AI and Data Science with the following mission:

  1. To be at the fore-front of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science research in all dimensions: theoretical research, application directed research, and locally relevant research for meeting regional needs
  2. To play a leading role in the delivery of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science education in formal and informal sectors at all levels.
  3. To become a globally coveted destination for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science technology development and entrepreneurship

To support the research ecosystem, School intends to set up Centres of Excellence on

  1. Brain Science and Applications
  2. Human Centered AI
  3. Mathematical and Computational Economics
  4. AI based Precision and Integrative Healthcare
  5. Intelligent Infrastructure
  6. Language Technology
  7. Guarantees for Machine Learning
