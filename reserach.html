Research Opportunities at IIT Jodhpur

M.S. (by Research) in Brain Science and Applications

The Centre of Excellence on Brain Science and Applications (CBSA) at IIT Jodhpur invites applications for its M.S. (by Research) program. This program focuses on brain-inspired next-generation AI, training students in computational techniques for brain function studies and real-world applications like language technology, computational vision, and brain-computer interfacing.


M.S. (by Research) in Computational Economics

The Centre for Mathematical and Computational Economics (CMCE) offers an M.S. (by Research) program that equips students with tools for economic modeling in a multidisciplinary setup. It covers auction mechanisms, platform economy, multi-agent games, and intersections with AI, finance, and supply chain.


M.S. (by Research) in Intelligent Infrastructure

The Centre of Excellence on Intelligent Infrastructure (CII) invites applications for its M.S. (by Research) program. This program focuses on intelligent infrastructure, incorporating AI & ML, building information modeling (BIM), smart sensing technologies, cyber-physical systems, and big data analytics.


M.S. (by Research) in Precision Health and Integrative Medicine

The Center of Excellence in AI-based Precision Healthcare offers an M.S. (by Research) program in Precision Health and Integrative Medicine. This program focuses on P5 (Predictive, Preventative, Personalized, Participatory, Promotive) medical specialties, integrating data sciences, AIOT, multi-omics, sensor technologies, and Ayurgenomics.
