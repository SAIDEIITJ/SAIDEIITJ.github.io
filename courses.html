Courses Offered for B.Tech., Masters, and Ph.D. students

The School of AIDE offers specialized courses as Open Electives (Table below) by different Centres-of-Excellence (CBSA, CMCE) for B.Tech., Masters and Ph.D. students. The courses are unique in terms of their interdisciplinarity nature across wide range while being highly specialized at the same time.

Some of these courses are carefully chosen and grouped for designing specialized Minor Programs (Links below). Eligibility: B.Tech. students of all branches are eligible to choose any minor program of interest. These are designed in a way that will allow you to begin the program in the 3rd year and complete it by the end of your graduation.
