Come learn with us!

At SAIDE, we offer a wide range of programs designed to provide students with a transformative learning experience that prepares them for the challenges and opportunities of the digital age. Our programs blend rigorous academic training, hands-on experience, and interdisciplinary collaboration to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to thrive in today's complex and interconnected world.

About our programs

  • Ph.D. in Complex Systems Science: Join our doctoral program in complex systems science and embark on a journey of discovery, innovation, and interdisciplinary research at the intersection of AI, DS, and complex systems theory.

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in AI and DS: Deepen your expertise in AI and DS with our graduate program, which offers advanced coursework, research opportunities, and industry collaborations to help you become a leader in the field.

  • Minor Programs in AI and DS: Complement your primary area of study with a minor in AI or DS, and gain valuable skills and knowledge that enhance your academic profile and career prospects.

  • Master of Science (M.S.) in AI and DS: Elevate your knowledge and skills with our master's program in AI and DS, designed for students seeking advanced training and specialization in areas such as machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and more.
